Our Process

  • Recruitment Planning / Requirement Review

    The first step involved in the recruitment process is planning. Here, planning involves to draft a comprehensive job specification for the vacant position, outlining its major and minor responsibilities; the skills, experience and qualifications needed; grade and level of pay; starting date; whether temporary or permanent. Or we review the requirement and job description given by our client.

  • Sourcing

    Sourcing candidates means our employment specialist is using a variety of methods to find suitable candidates from all over the world for the job vacancies. Sourcing can be done via HumanCapita internal database, job portals, online advertising, career sites and professional networking.

  • Pre-screening

    Conducting a pre screening is essential for obtaining information about the applicantâ€™s background, work history and experience. Our employment specialist conducts either preliminary phone interview or face to face interview; the objective is to determine whether or not the applicant has the requisite skills and qualifications for the job vacancy.

  • Client assessment

    The profiles of short listed candidates will be sending to our client for final assessment and selection. And client will select the candidate at their discretion.

  • Coordinating Interview

    We will be coordinating with our client to conduct the final face to face interview of the selected candidate.

  • Reference check

    We will take the initiative to conduct necessary reference check of the selected candidate.

  • Deployment & Post Deployment Services

    We assist our client to deploy the employee on the time and we also provide minimum one month of guarantee period to replace the employee.
