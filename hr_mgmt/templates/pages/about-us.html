About HumanCapita

HumanCapita is a premier global recruitment agency headquartered in Singapore.
HumanCapita provides the full spectrum of recruitment services for all type of industries all over the world. We specialise in providing the best suitable candidates for both permanent and contractual positions.

We recognise the rapid changing demands for highly qualified professionals and we offer exceptional and efficient solutions to meet our clients needs. Our qualified executive team monitors the current trends in the job market. We work with our clients to fulfill their manpower requirements and offer customised HR solutions. Through our updated data base, we have a pool of diverse and readily available professionals. We take a holistic approach to our solution, starting from scheduling interviews right up to deployment of our candidates.

We understand how crucial it is to have prompt provision of effective, efficient and specialised personnel in all sectors. Our resources and proficiency are focused on the following sectors, where we deliver our solutions in the most professional and timely manner.

  • IT and Telecom
  • Healthcare
  • Banking & finance
  • Education
  • Pharmaceutical
  • Hospitality
  • Marine
  • Sales & Marketing
  • Oil and Gas
  • Engineering
  • Building & Construction
  • Digital Media

Our highly qualified executive team comprises of industry experts who have experience in deploying and managing employees across APAC and Singapore.
