About HumanCapita

HumanCapita is a premier global recruitment agency headquartered in Singapore.
HumanCapita provides the full spectrum of recruitment services for all type of industries all over the world. We specialise in providing the best suitable candidates for both permanent and contractual positions.

We recognize the rapid changing demands for the highly qualified professionals and we offer an exceptional service in limited time frames to meet our customer needs. Our qualified executive team will monitor the current trends in the job market and will work with our clients to fulfill all their manpower requirements and to provide the best one stop HR solutions. Through our large pool of data base, we guarantee ready availability of ideal professionals for every position of our specialty sectors. Everything from the planning to the interview, leading to selection, and reference check, right up to their joining and performance is our responsibility.

We understand that how crucial it is to have prompt provision of effective, efficient and specialised personnel for all sectors.
